About

The Systems Research Group focuses on designing, building, and evaluating computer systems that are efficient, reliable, and scalable. Our research spans multiple areas including distributed systems, operating systems, computer architecture, and system security.

We tackle fundamental challenges in modern computing systems, from cloud infrastructure and edge computing to emerging hardware architectures and system software. Our work combines theoretical insights with practical system building, resulting in both academic contributions and real-world impact.

Our interdisciplinary approach brings together expertise in computer science, electrical engineering, and applied mathematics to address complex system challenges. We collaborate closely with industry partners and contribute to open-source projects that benefit the broader computing community.

Research

Our research addresses fundamental challenges in modern computing systems, with a focus on scalability, reliability, and performance. We work on both theoretical foundations and practical system implementations.

Distributed Systems

We design and build large-scale distributed systems that can handle massive workloads while maintaining consistency and fault tolerance. Our work includes consensus algorithms, distributed databases, and microservices architectures.

Operating Systems

Our OS research focuses on next-generation kernel designs, container technologies, and system virtualization. We explore new abstractions and mechanisms for efficient resource management and isolation.

Computer Architecture

We investigate novel computer architectures including heterogeneous computing, memory hierarchies, and specialized accelerators. Our work bridges the gap between hardware capabilities and software requirements.

System Security

Our security research addresses vulnerabilities in system software, secure system design, and privacy-preserving computing. We develop both defensive mechanisms and formal verification techniques.

Edge Computing

We explore computing at the network edge, focusing on resource-constrained environments, latency-sensitive applications, and the coordination between edge and cloud resources.

Storage Systems

Our storage research covers distributed storage, persistent memory, and emerging storage technologies. We work on both system-level optimizations and application-aware storage solutions.
